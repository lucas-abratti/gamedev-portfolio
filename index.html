About Me

Hi! I'm a game developer with professional experience in Unity, Godot, and I am also familiar with Unreal and GameMaker. I love solving technical challenges, thats why I chose programming as my job and passion but I enjoy learning every aspect of game development, on my own or with help and mentoring from my collegues.

I’ve worked on a wide range of game projects—from online multiplayer (like a 1v1 shooter with roguelike progression and a co-op Barbuta remake) to procedural puzzle games and deconstructing classics like DOOM, Mario, and Rogue. Along the way, I’ve picked up just enough ‘programmer art’ skills (2D/3D, sound design with Audacity and BFXR) to build polished prototypes solo. Right now, I’m focused on sharpening my game design intuition—studying how small tweaks to mechanics or pacing can transform player experience.

Right now, I’m deep into the world of shmups—a genre often considered ‘solved’ by masterpieces like DoDonPachi, Mushihimesama, and Deathsmiles. What fascinates me is how these games balance razor-sharp mechanics with spectacle, and how their devoted community dissects every detail (from bullet patterns to scoring systems). I’m prototyping my own takes, iterating based on feedback from players who live and breathe these games.
